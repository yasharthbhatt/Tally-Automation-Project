# 3-Minute Hindi Video Script for Indian Businesses
## StockSense - Smart Inventory Intelligence for Tally Users

**Target Audience:** Indian traders, distributors, wholesalers using Tally
**Duration:** ~3 minutes
**Language:** Hindi (with English subtitles option)
**Style:** Professional yet relatable, problem-solution format

---

## VIDEO STRUCTURE

### SCENE 1: OPENING - रात की चिंता (0:00-0:25)
**Duration:** 25 seconds

**Visual:**
- Night scene, businessman at desk with laptop, worried expression
- Stock registers, Tally reports scattered
- Clock showing 11:30 PM
- Phone showing missed calls from customers

**Narration (Hindi):**
```
क्या आपको भी रात में नींद नहीं आती?
स्टॉक खत्म हो जाएगा कि नहीं?
कौन सा प्रोडक्ट कब ऑर्डर करना है?
कौन से ग्राहक आपसे दूर जा रहे हैं?

Tally में सारा डेटा है... लेकिन जवाब कहाँ हैं?
```

**Text Overlay (Hindi + English):**
- "क्या आपको भी यह परेशानी है?"
- "Are you facing these problems?"

**Background Music:** Subtle, tense (धीमा, तनाव भरा)

## PRODUCTION NOTES

### Voice Over Requirements:

**Voice Type:**
- Male, 35-45 age range
- Clear Hindi diction
- Relatable, not overly formal
- Energy level: Moderate-high
- Pace: 140-150 words/minute

**Alternative:**
- Female voice (professional, trustworthy)
- Or mix: Problem (concerned tone) → Solution (confident tone)

### Visual Style:

**Color Scheme:**
- Primary: Deep Blue (#1a237e) - trust, professionalism
- Accent: Orange (#ff6f00) - energy, action
- Success: Green (#2e7d32) - profit, growth
- Alert: Red (#c62828) - urgency

**Typography:**
- Hindi: Noto Sans Devanagari (clear, modern)
- English: Montserrat or Poppins
- Size: Large enough for mobile viewing

**Animation Style:**
- Clean, professional
- Not too flashy (target audience is traditional businesses)
- Focus on clarity over creativity

### B-Roll Footage Suggestions:

1. Indian warehouse/godown scenes
2. Small business owners working
3. Tally software screenshots
4. Mobile phone usage
5. Traditional Indian market scenes
6. Products: Rice bags, oil containers, wheat, grains
7. Ledger books, calculators
8. Happy customers shopping
9. Business meetings

### Music Recommendations:

**Opening:** Thoughtful, slightly tense
**Problem:** Neutral, concerned
**Solution:** Uplifting, modern
**Features:** Corporate, professional
**Impact:** Triumphant, success
**CTA:** Energetic, motivating

**Cultural Note:** Avoid overly Western music. Use fusion or Indian instruments subtly.

## TESTING RECOMMENDATIONS

Before finalizing:

1. **Show to 3-5 actual Tally users** (your target audience)
2. **Test comprehension:** Do they understand the benefits?
3. **Cultural check:** Any phrases that feel off?
4. **Pace check:** Too fast? Too slow?
5. **CTA clarity:** Do they know what to do next?
