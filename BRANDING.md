# 🧠 StockSense Brand Guidelines

## Brand Identity

**Name:** StockSense
**Tagline:** Smart Inventory. Smarter Business.
**Icon:** 🧠 (Brain emoji)

---

## Brand Story

StockSense represents the intelligence layer between your Tally data and business decisions. The "brain" symbolizes AI-powered thinking, while "Sense" conveys both intelligence and the ability to detect patterns and predict outcomes.

---

## Visual Identity

### Logo
- **Primary:** 🧠 StockSense
- **Colors:**
  - Primary: Deep Blue (#1a237e) - Trust, professionalism
  - Accent: Orange (#ff6f00) - Energy, action, alerts
  - Success: Green (#2e7d32) - Growth, profit
  - Alert: Red (#c62828) - Urgency, warnings

### Typography
- **Headers:** Montserrat Bold or Poppins SemiBold
- **Body:** Inter Regular or Roboto
- **Code/Data:** Fira Code or JetBrains Mono

---

## Voice & Tone

### Personality
- **Intelligent** but not intimidating
- **Professional** but approachable
- **Confident** but humble
- **Action-oriented** not theoretical

### Language Guidelines

**Do Say:**
- "Smart Inventory. Smarter Business."
- "Know exactly when to reorder"
- "Your AI inventory assistant"
- "Predict stockouts before they happen"
- "Transform data into decisions"

**Don't Say:**
- "Revolutionary" (overused)
- "Complex AI algorithms" (too technical)
- "World-class" (generic)
- "Cutting-edge technology" (cliché)

### Example Messaging

**Product Description (Short):**
"StockSense is an AI-powered inventory intelligence platform for Tally users. Get demand forecasts, customer insights, and smart reorder recommendations in under 30 seconds."

**Product Description (Long):**
"StockSense transforms your Tally data into actionable intelligence. Using AI and machine learning, it predicts product demand, identifies at-risk customers, calculates optimal reorder quantities, and provides a natural language assistant you can ask questions in plain English or Hindi. Built specifically for Indian traders and distributors, StockSense helps you prevent stockouts, optimize cash flow, and grow your business with data-driven decisions."

**Elevator Pitch:**
"StockSense is like having a business analyst and data scientist working 24/7 on your inventory. Just upload your Tally exports, and our AI tells you exactly what to order, when to order it, and which customers need attention - all in under 30 seconds."
